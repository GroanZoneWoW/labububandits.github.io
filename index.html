Welcome, Bandit!

Welcome to Labubu Bandits! We are a band of adventurers, misfits, and champions carving our legend into the world of Ambershire. Whether you're a seasoned veteran of Azeroth or a fresh face eager for glory, you may find a home among us.

Our Saga

The Labubu Bandits were founded by a group of friends with a shared vision. We sought to create a guild that valued community and progression in equal measure. We believe that the greatest loot is the friendships we forge and the memories we create while conquering Azeroth's most formidable foes.

What We Do

  • Raiding: We plan on starting running pugs until we have a crew together and we will have dedicated raid nights where we tackle content like Molten Core, Blackwing Lair and beyond. Expect music (discord DJ bot) and fun.
  • Dungeons: We are always running dungeons together, whether it's for pre-raid gear, attunements, or just for fun.
  • PvP: From spontaneous world PvP to organized Battlegrounds, the Labubu Bandits are always ready to fight.

Answer the Call!

The Labubu Bandits are always looking for strong, dedicated players to bolster our ranks. If you are passionate about Turtle WoW, committed to teamwork, and eager to conquer challenges, we want you!
